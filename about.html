About MediCare Hospital

Learn about our history, mission, and the team that makes it all possible.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, MediCare Hospital began as a small community clinic with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to all. Over the years, we've grown into a state-of-the-art medical facility serving thousands of patients annually.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous accolades, including the prestigious "Hospital of the Year" award three times in the past decade.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence and compassionate care.

Our Mission

To improve the health of our community by providing exceptional, patient-centered care through innovation and teamwork.

Our Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Teamwork guide everything we do.
